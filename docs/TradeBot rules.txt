The TradeBot system operates on a professional risk management framework designed for consistency and protection. Here are the core rules, risk-reward parameters, and strategy logic implemented in your system:

📊 Risk Management Rules
The bot follows a "Safety First" approach with hard-coded constraints to protect your capital:

Risk/Reward Ratio: 1:2 (Default).
Trade Target: Rs 2,000 per trade (Fixed amount).
Trade Stop Loss (SL): Rs 1,000 per trade (Fixed amount).
Daily Trade Limit: Max 2 trades per day. The bot stops automatically after 2 trades (regardless of profit or loss) to prevent overtrading.
Daily Kill-Switch: If the total account loss exceeds 15% in a single day, the bot halts all activities immediately for the rest of the day.
📉 Trailing Stop Loss (4-Level Ladder)
Your bot uses a progressive Laddered TSL to lock in profits while giving the trade room to hit the full target:

Level 1 (30% of target reached): SL is moved to Entry Price (Break-even). You can no longer lose money on the trade.
Level 2 (50% of target reached): SL locks in 30% of target profit.
Level 3 (75% of target reached): SL locks in 50% of target profit.
Level 4 (90% of target reached): SL locks in 75% of target profit.
🧩 Trading Strategy (Combined Signal)
The bot uses a Consensus Model (2-out-of-3) to ensure high-probability entries. A trade is only taken if at least two of the following strategies agree on the direction:

EMA Crossover (9/21):
Bullish: 9-period EMA crosses above 21-period EMA.
Bearish: 9-period EMA crosses below 21-period EMA.
Candle Breakout & Volume Spike:
Monitors the High/Low of the previous 5-minute candle.
Requires a Volume Spike (current volume > 1.5x average volume) during the breakout.
RSI (Relative Strength Index):
Bullish: RSI is above 60.
Bearish: RSI is below 40.
⏰ Operational Regulations
Trading Window: New trades are only permitted between 9:30 AM and 2:30 PM IST.
Restricted Zone: No trades are taken during the low-volume "Lunch Zone" (12:00 PM – 1:30 PM).
Auto-Squareoff: All open positions are forcibly closed at 3:10 PM to avoid overnight risk.
Option Selection: The bot automatically selects ATM (At-The-Money) strike prices for the nearest weekly expiry to ensure maximum liquidity.
